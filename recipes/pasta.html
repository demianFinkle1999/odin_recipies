Basic Pasta Sauce

  • Pasta (dealers choice)
  • Protien (also dealers choice)
  • Veggies.. Gotta have!
  • Can of tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Herbs i.e. basil, thyme, rosemary

      You can expierment with diffrent herbs with your dishes. Though I recommend doing a little reserch with what herbs go with what protiens.
  • Patience
  1. Cook protien to a rare tempature. Don't worry it will be done by the time the dish is done
  2. Remove protien and in the oil/grease cook down veggies
  3. Add garlic and herbs and sautee for 5-8 mins or until fraagrent.
  4. Add can of crushed tomatoes and sauce and reduce till thick
  5. Cook Past till al dente or crispy on edges
  6. Add to sauce
  7. Put back protien aand cook untill pasta sticks to pasta about 30 mins
